What is Bridg-it?

Also known as Game of Gale or Shannon switching game, it is a game where two players attempt to construct a path from one side of the board to the opposite side.

Obs:

  • If the black player does not react its your turn again.
  • You can unselect a dot by clicking on it again.
Black player wins!

Rules:

  • Black goes first.
  • Each turn a player can connect two adjacent dots if there's no line separating them.
  • Red wins if he can construct a path from the top to the bottom of the board.
  • Black wins if he can construct a path connecting the left and right sides.
